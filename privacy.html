Privacy Policy

How Taunet Nelel collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: June 2025

Who we are

Taunet Nelel is a community-based organisation in Victoria, Australia. Contact us at info@taunetnelel.org or +61 475 273 985.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, and membership details when you register, contact us, subscribe to updates, or attend events.

How we use your information

We use your information to manage membership, communicate about events and community activities, process enquiries, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Storage & security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access. Member data is stored securely and accessed only by authorised volunteers and committee members.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. You may unsubscribe from event emails at any time.

Third-party links

Our website may link to social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp). Those services have their own privacy policies.
